What We Do

At Waltz-Green, we believe that every space tells a story. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between property acquisition and stunning, purposeful design, we combine real estate expertise with creative interior solutions to help you find, shape, and love the spaces you live and work in.

Real Estate Services

From residential to commercial properties, we provide end-to-end real estate solutions including buying, selling, leasing, and investment consulting. Our team guides you through every step of the property journey with integrity and transparency.

Interior Design

Our design philosophy blends aesthetics with functionality. We create customized interior experiences that reflect your style, meet your needs, and optimize your space. From concept to completion, we focus on detail, harmony, and lasting appeal.

Space Planning & Remodelling

We reimagine existing spaces to unlock their full potential. Whether it's a complete transformation or a subtle refresh, our remodelling and space planning services are tailored to enhance flow, efficiency, and beauty. We work with both residential and commercial clients to ensure every square foot works smartly and stylishly.
